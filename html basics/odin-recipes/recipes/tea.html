Tea Recipe

Description

Delight in a warm and soothing cup of tea with this simple yet satisfying recipe. Tea has been enjoyed for centuries and is known for its calming properties and delightful flavors. Whether you're starting your day or taking a moment to relax, a perfect cup of tea can be a wonderful companion.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tea bag or 1 teaspoon loose tea leaves
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • Milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Bring the water to a boil in a kettle or saucepan.
  2. Place the tea bag or loose tea leaves in a teapot or cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the tea and let it steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on desired strength.
  4. If desired, add sugar to taste and stir until dissolved.
  5. For a creamy tea, add a splash of milk.
  6. Give it a gentle stir, and your delicious cup of tea is ready to enjoy!
